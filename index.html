About

Light in the right hands.

"I don't chase the light — I find it where no one else is looking."

Luz by LJ is the photography work of LJ Jaramillo, born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Rooted in low-light portraiture, cinematic framing, and the raw energy of the Southwest, LJ's work finds the extraordinary in ordinary moments — alleyways lit by a single bulb, neon signs bleeding color into the dark, faces illuminated by the world around them.


Available for portrait sessions, commercial campaigns, and live event coverage across New Mexico and beyond.
