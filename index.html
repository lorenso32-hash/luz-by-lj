About

Light in the right hands.

Visual & Creative Director

Luz by LJ is the work of LJ — a Santa Fe-based visual artist and photographer born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Rooted in low-light portraiture, cinematic framing, and the raw energy of the Southwest, LJ finds the extraordinary in ordinary moments — alleyways lit by a single bulb, neon signs bleeding color into the dark, faces illuminated by the world around them.


Beyond the still frame, LJ brings the same eye to music video production, short-form content, and curated live performance coverage — translating energy into image across every format. Based in Santa Fe and available throughout New Mexico and the surrounding Southwest.
